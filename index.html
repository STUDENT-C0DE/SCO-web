About Us

The Student Council Organization serves as the official bridge between students and the Tay Tung school administration. We are dedicated to representing student interests, advocating for meaningful policy changes, and fostering a vibrant school culture through high-impact events and community service. By empowering student voices and providing a platform for leadership, we work to ensure that every individual on campus feels heard, valued, and connected to the broader school community.
