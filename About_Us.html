About Us

The Student Council Organization serves as the official bridge between students and the Tay Tung school administration. We are dedicated to representing student interests, advocating for meaningful policy changes, and fostering a vibrant school culture through high-impact events and community service. By empowering student voices and providing a platform for leadership, we work to ensure that every individual on campus feels heard, valued, and connected to the broader school community.


Our Values


Subsidiarity

We believe in empowering students at every level to take initiative. By practicing subsidiarity, we ensure that decisions are made by those most affected, encouraging individual committees and grade levels to lead their own projects and solve problems independently.

Solidarity

Our organization is built on unity and mutual support. We advocate for the collective well-being of the student body, ensuring that every voice is included and that we act as a cohesive force to foster a campus environment where no one is left behind.

Leadership

We view leadership as a commitment to service and integrity. Our goal is to inspire our peers through ethical action and proactive vision, modeling the way for others to lead with character while working toward the continuous improvement of our school community.




About Tay Tung


Mission, Vision, and Philisophy


Philisophy

Insired by the teachings of Confucious, Bacolod Tay Tung Highschool, INC. believes in the tenets of a geat harmony, advocating for teaching and learning as a lifelong process of self improvement, cultivating good moral character and developing virtues.

Mission

Bacolod Tay Tung Highschool, INC. equips learners with the knowledge, skills, and virtues, necessary to adapt, innovate, and lead with integrity to succeed in a dynamic and evolving globalized world.

Vision

Bacolod Tay Tung Highchool Inc. is a premier Filipino-Chinese intitution distinguished for good moral character formation and lifelong learning.
